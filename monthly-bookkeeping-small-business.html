Common Questions

Monthly bookkeeping — what to know.

How much does monthly bookkeeping cost for a small business?

Pricing is flat-rate and based on your transaction volume and service requirements. After a free 20-minute call, you'll receive a clear fixed monthly quote — no hourly billing, no surprises.

What accounting software do you use?

We work across all major cloud accounting platforms — QuickBooks Online, Xero, Wave, Sage, Zoho Books, and FreshBooks. If you already have a platform, we'll work with it. If you're starting fresh, we'll recommend the right fit for your business.

Do I need to send you anything each month?

We keep the process as low-friction as possible. Once we're connected to your accounts, most months require very little from you. Occasionally we'll need clarification on a transaction — but you're not doing the bookkeeping, we are.

What if I'm also working with an accountant?

That's ideal. Bookkeeping and accounting are complementary — we handle the ongoing monthly records, your accountant handles corporate tax, year-end filings, and planning. We'll make sure your accountant gets clean, organized books when they need them.

Can I start mid-year?

Yes. We can take over your books at any point during the year. We'll review what's been done to date, address any issues, and establish a clean baseline going forward.

Ready to get your books in order?

No pressure — just a quick conversation to see how we can help.
