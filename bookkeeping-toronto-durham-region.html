Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to be located in Toronto or Durham Region to work with Solidus?

No. Solidus is remote-first and works with businesses across Canada and the United States. That said, we have strong familiarity with Ontario-based businesses and CRA requirements for businesses operating in Ontario.

How does remote bookkeeping work?

We connect to your cloud accounting platform — QuickBooks, Xero, Wave, or similar — and handle everything securely online. You share access to your accounts, we manage the books, and you receive clean monthly reports. No in-person meetings required.

What does bookkeeping cost for a small business in Ontario?

We offer flat monthly pricing based on your transaction volume and service needs. After a free 20-minute call, you'll receive a clear, fixed quote — no hourly rates, no surprise invoices.

Can you help if my books are behind or messy?

Yes — catch-up and cleanup bookkeeping is one of our core services. We'll assess the situation, give you an honest estimate, and bring everything up to date systematically and correctly.

Do you handle HST filing or payroll?

We support you in keeping your records organized and accurate so HST filing is straightforward. For payroll, get in touch and we can discuss your specific situation and what support makes sense.

Ready to get your books in order?

No pressure — just a quick conversation to see how we can help.
