Meet the Founder

Usman Khalid
Founder & Director — Solidus Accounting Inc.

Usman's background spans public accounting and advisory work across firms in the United Kingdom and Canada, as well as experience in the technology industry — giving him a practical understanding of how businesses at different stages actually operate.

He holds the ACCA designation and is a certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor. He founded Solidus to give small and growing businesses access to professional-grade accounting support — without the overhead, complexity, or unpredictable billing that comes with larger firms.

Solidus was founded on a simple idea: small businesses deserve the same quality of financial support as larger ones — delivered clearly, priced fairly, and without the friction.
